Types of Pumps We Install

  • Centrifugal Pumps
    A dependable choice for everyday use such as tank filling, water transfer, and general supply.
  • Submersible Pumps (Borehole Pumps)
    Installed directly inside boreholes and wells, these pumps are ideal for deep water sources and provide efficient, steady flow.
  • Booster Pumps
    Designed to improve low water pressure in buildings, ensuring smooth flow in taps, bathrooms, and irrigation systems.
  • Solar Water Pumps
    Cost-effective and eco-friendly systems powered by solar energy—perfect for rural and off-grid locations.
  • Jet Pumps
    Suitable for shallow and deep wells, commonly used in residential setups where boreholes are not too deep.
  • High-Pressure Pumps
    Built for demanding applications such as irrigation, commercial use, and areas requiring strong water output.
