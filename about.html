About GrantedWells Ltd

Leading provider of water solutions in Kenya

Founded in 2016, GrantedWells Ltd has been at the forefront of providing reliable water solutions across Kenya. With over 10 years of experience and 500+ successful installations, we specialize in pump installation, water purification, distillation systems, and comprehensive maintenance services.

Our team of certified technicians ensures high-quality workmanship and durable equipment. We serve both residential and commercial clients in Nairobi and surrounding regions.
